Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

  • Is it the right time to build my dream home?

    If you're considering constructing your dream home, it's advisable not to delay. The costs of building materials and labour are consistently rising each month. Opting to build your home this year could result in savings of 10–15% compared to waiting until the next year.

  • Do you provide support to get electricity and water connections from the government?

    Certainly, obtaining electricity and water connections involves straightforward legal procedures that we can efficiently handle. Our team of experts is equipped to manage these formalities on your behalf, ensuring a smooth process for you and minimising any potential challenges during the construction of your house.

  • Do you provide approved drawings and help with government permission to build the house?

    Absolutely. When you choose to build with us, our dedicated team will handle all the paperwork, sparing you the hassle of dealing with government approvals.

  • Do you provide 3D models before construction begins?

    Yes, all our projects come with 3D model and complete visualization for you so that you can be sure of what we are building.

  • Can I only avail of your architecture and interior design services?

    Yes. We do undertake these services separately, if required.

  • Do you construct a swimming pool?

    Yes, we make customised swimming pools with all the necessary features you desire.

  • Do you provide termite and water proofing?

    Yes, we are very concerned about quality, and these are already included in the pricing.

  • Do you provide support to get home loans?

    Yes, we can take you through the correct channels so that you can get a home loan easily without facing unwanted situations.

  • How Long Will It Take To Handover The Key?

    It depends on the site conditions and the work. The approximate duration for a 2500-square-foot house would be 6–12 months.
